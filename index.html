  • About Us

    We are 3 students whose main goal is to help locals in Sonoma County in an event of a natural disaster. Our website, Geo Shelters, is a simulation of all the shelters that helped Sonoma County to recover from the fire. We have chosen 25 shelters to be represented on the map. Each shelter has information regarding capacity, assets offered, and help needed. A special feature of our website is the ability to see the amount of people at a shelter at any given time, which is updated by admins of each shelter.

    The recent Fire taught us a lot about how we, as residents of Sonoma County, need to be ready to respond to any natural disaster that may come in the future. Our website was created so that anyone can easily locate shelters in their area.
